# Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally lightweight and evolves with operational learning.

## Near Term

- Consolidate module-level notes, experiments, and diagrams.
- Establish repeatable experiment patterns with clear validation steps.

## Mid Term

- Expand reliability practices with controlled failure testing.
- Build cross-module operational references as they emerge.

## Long Term

- Mature observability into SLO-driven operational workflows.
- Develop automation patterns that reduce operational toil.
